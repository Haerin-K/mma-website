About This Site

Multimedia and authoring combines visual design, motion, sound, interactivity, and storytelling into finished digital experiences. This site documents that process through practical production work, showing how ideas move from early experiments into polished media pieces.

The weekly progress pages record technical decisions, creative revisions, and production milestones. The final output pages collect completed work so each project can be viewed as both a finished artifact and the result of an intentional authoring workflow.
